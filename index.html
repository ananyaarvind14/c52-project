Interesting Facts about Chameleon

>
  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of the communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to rap around tree branches
  • They come in different sizes

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

>
  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navagational abilities
  3. Pigeons have incredible hearing abilities
  4. They produce there own milk for their child but be aware drinking their milk could kill you

Interesting Facts about Camels

>
  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

    >
  • They have great camouflage for their environment
  • Their eyes are different to normal bears.
  • Cubs are well protected in their first month.
